## <a name="active-replication"></a><span data-ttu-id="220c0-162">능동 복제</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="220c0-162">Active replication</span></span>

## <a name="passive-replication"></a><span data-ttu-id="220c0-174">수동 복제</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="220c0-174">Passive replication</span></span>
